Key Legislations (2020-2024)

A summary of significant bills and their outcomes.

Passed

Nari Shakti Vandan Adhiniyam (Women's Reservation Bill)

The Constitution (106th Amendment) Act, 2023, reserving one-third of all seats in Lok Sabha and State Legislative Assemblies for women. This also applies to seats reserved for SCs/STs.

Tabled by: Arjun Ram Meghwal (Minister of Law)
Year: 2023
Lok Sabha (LS): Passed (Votes: 454 Yes / 2 No)
Rajya Sabha (RS): Passed (Votes: 214 Yes / 0 No)
Passed

Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023

Establishes the rules for the processing of digital personal data. It grants individuals rights over their data and creates obligations for entities (Data Fiduciaries) that process data.

Tabled by: Ashwini Vaishnaw (Minister of Electronics & IT)
Year: 2023
Lok Sabha (LS): Passed (Voice Vote)
Rajya Sabha (RS): Passed (Voice Vote)
Repealed

Farm Laws Repeal Act, 2021

This Act formally repealed the three controversial farm laws passed by Parliament in 2020, following widespread and sustained protests by farmer unions.

Tabled by: Narendra Singh Tomar (Minister of Agriculture)
Year: 2021
Lok Sabha (LS): Passed (Voice Vote)
Rajya Sabha (RS): Passed (Voice Vote)
